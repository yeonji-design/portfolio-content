    Snagit: Creating new interactive video markup tool

    What is Snagit?

    Snagit is screen capture and screen recording software for Windows and macOS. It is created and developed by TechSmith and was first launched in 1990.

    Problem

    How might we make communication easier through interactive video markup tools?

    Research Findings

    Our target users group are those who do not have that much video editing skills, so keeping it as ‘simple to use’ as possible is important. Also, they wanted fun & entertaining tools to engage their viewers. Finally, Users want to draw viewers’ attention to specific content. Based on this research findings, we designed persona of our desired solution. Our main persona would be a person who is not very experienced with video editing. In addition, they would be a type of person who work on short videos either for work or entertainment. They would also mainly use it for internal and external communication at workplace

    Ideation

    Using crazy 8’s we started brainstorming and sketched different ideas based on key findings and goals. Different levels of low fidelity sketches were created to test out our concept and assumptions. Fun & Entertaining: Including stickers and emojis to include an element of joy. Simple & Easy to Use: Eliminating all but the most essential markup tools. Combining multiple functions and using single track timeline. Directing Viewer's Focus: Developing markup tools (arrows, text and pen) that guide viewers' attention to specific content.
